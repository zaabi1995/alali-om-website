Defense & Security

Trusted supply solutions for Oman's Royal Armed Forces and national security institutions.

Supplying Oman's Defense Sector

Alali Investment has been a trusted procurement partner for Oman's Royal Armed Forces and national security institutions for over a decade. We understand the critical importance of reliability, speed, and uncompromising quality when it comes to defense supply chains. Every product we source meets stringent quality standards, and every delivery is executed with the precision that mission-critical operations demand.

From tactical vehicles and ground support equipment to personal protective gear and communication systems, we provide end-to-end procurement solutions tailored to the specific needs of each branch of the Royal Armed Forces, the Royal Oman Police, and associated security agencies.

Confidentiality & Compliance

We handle all defense procurement with the highest levels of confidentiality, compliance, and discretion, adhering strictly to Oman's procurement regulations and international standards.
