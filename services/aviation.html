Our Services

Aviation

Comprehensive ground support, parts supply, and aviation services for Oman's civil and military aviation sector.

Supporting Oman's Aviation Industry

Alali Investment serves Oman's aviation sector with a comprehensive portfolio of ground support equipment, aircraft consumables, maintenance supplies, and airport operational products. Our deep understanding of aviation procurement requirements — combined with our extensive supplier network — ensures that our clients receive exactly what they need, on time and to specification.

We supply aviation-grade tires from Dunlop — trusted by airlines and air forces worldwide — alongside electrical testing equipment from Fluke, lighting solutions for airfield operations, and a broad range of operational consumables. For both civil aviation operators like Oman Air and military clients like RAFO, we deliver with the precision and reliability that aviation demands.

Aviation-Grade Quality Assurance

All aviation supplies are sourced from approved manufacturers and come with full certification and traceability documentation required by aviation authorities.
