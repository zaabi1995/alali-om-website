Our Services

Construction & Infrastructure

Heavy equipment, materials, and supply solutions for Oman's construction and infrastructure development.

Building Oman's Future

As Oman accelerates its infrastructure development under Vision 2040, Alali Investment is proud to be a key supply partner for the construction sector. We provide world-class heavy equipment from Caterpillar, Hitachi, JCB, and Volvo, alongside high-performance construction tires from Dunlop, Firemax, GITI, Goodride, Primewell, and Dana — ensuring that construction fleets operate at peak efficiency on Oman's most demanding project sites.

Our portfolio covers the full construction supply spectrum: from earth-moving machinery and cranes to generators, electrical systems, and precision testing instruments. We understand that downtime costs money, which is why we maintain responsive supply chains and carry substantial inventory to meet urgent site requirements without delay.

Trusted Global Brands

We are authorized distributors and resellers of Caterpillar, Hitachi, JCB, Volvo, Dunlop, Fuji Electric, Sumitomo, and other world-class brands for the Oman market.
