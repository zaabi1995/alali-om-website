Our Services

Renewable Energy

Solar, power storage, and electrical supply solutions supporting Oman's clean energy transition under Vision 2040.

Powering Oman's Green Future

Oman's Vision 2040 commits the nation to significant growth in renewable energy generation and sustainable development. Alali Investment is actively positioned to support this transformation by supplying world-class components, systems, and equipment for solar power installations, battery energy storage systems (BESS), and smart electrical infrastructure across government and private sector projects.

Our renewable energy portfolio leverages strategic partnerships with leading brands: Fuji Electric for advanced power electronics and energy management systems, Xtreme Power for reliable battery storage solutions, Sumitomo for high-quality electrical cables and components, and Fluke for precision testing of photovoltaic and electrical systems. We ensure every project receives components that meet international renewable energy standards.

Supporting Oman Vision 2040

We are committed to supporting Oman's national goal of generating 30% of its electricity from renewable sources by 2030, contributing through world-class supply and technical support.
