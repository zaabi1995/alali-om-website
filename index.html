About Alali Investment

Alali Investment is a Muscat-based trading and procurement company serving as a strategic bridge between Oman's most prominent government and corporate entities and the world's most reputable international suppliers.

Our expertise spans across ten critical industry sectors, enabling us to deliver end-to-end procurement, supply chain, and project support services. From defense and aviation to renewable energy and marine, we bring global-standard capabilities to every engagement.
