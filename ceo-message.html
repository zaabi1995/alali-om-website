CEO Message

A message from the founder and Chief Executive Officer of Alali Investment.

AG

Ali Ghalib Alsaid

Chief Executive Officer

Founder, Alali Investment

Trust is not earned in a single transaction — it is built over years of consistent excellence.

When we founded Alali Investment, our vision was clear: to build a company that Oman's most demanding institutions could rely on unconditionally. From our earliest days, we set out not merely to trade products, but to deliver trust — trust in the quality of what we supply, trust in the commitments we make, and trust in our ability to stand alongside our partners through every challenge they face.

Over more than a decade of continuous service, I have had the privilege of working alongside some of Oman's most distinguished organizations — the Royal Air Force of Oman, the Royal Navy of Oman, the Royal Army of Oman, the Royal Oman Police, ASYAD Group, OQ, Oman Air, Mwasalat, and many others. Each partnership has deepened our understanding of what true service means: listening carefully, responding swiftly, and delivering without compromise. These relationships are the foundation upon which Alali Investment has grown, and they are the reason we wake up each morning determined to do better.

Our core values — integrity, quality, and reliability — are not aspirational statements printed in an annual report. They are the standards by which every member of our team is held, every supplier is selected, and every delivery is judged. We do not cut corners. We do not compromise on quality. We do not make promises we cannot keep. This discipline has earned us the trust of institutions that accept nothing less than the best, and it is a discipline we will never abandon.

Oman's Vision 2040 charts an inspiring and ambitious path for our nation's economic diversification, industrial development, and human empowerment. At Alali Investment, we see ourselves not as bystanders to this transformation, but as active contributors. By connecting world-class global brands — from Caterpillar and Volvo to Fuji Electric and Fluke — with Oman's most strategic sectors, we help build the capabilities, the infrastructure, and the self-sufficiency that will power our nation's future. I warmly invite you to join us on this journey. Together, let us build something enduring.

Warm regards,

Ali Ghalib Alsaid

Chief Executive Officer

Alali Investment | Muscat, Sultanate of Oman
