Ali Ghalib Alsaid

Chief Executive Officer

Founder

Ali Ghalib Alsaid founded Alali Investment with a clear mission: to create a trading and supply company that Oman's most demanding institutions could depend on unconditionally. His leadership philosophy centers on building lasting relationships through uncompromising quality, transparent business practices, and a deep commitment to Oman's strategic development goals. Ali Adnan Al-Zaabi co-founded Alali Investment bringing a wealth of expertise in business development, international sourcing, and supplier relations.
