Company Overview

Alali Investment is a Muscat-based trading and procurement company serving as a strategic bridge between Oman's most prominent government and corporate entities and the world's most reputable international suppliers.

Our expertise spans across ten critical industry sectors, enabling us to deliver end-to-end procurement, supply chain, and project support services. From defense and aviation to renewable energy and marine, we bring global-standard capabilities to every engagement. With a growing portfolio of trusted brand partnerships and a proven track record with Oman's leading institutions, Alali continues to expand its footprint as a reliable partner for complex procurement and special projects.

Our Vision

To stand as Oman's most trusted gateway for global suppliers and customers — connecting the world's finest products with Oman's growing industries.

Our Mission

To deliver excellence in procurement, supply, and project execution across all sectors, with unwavering commitment to quality, compliance, and long-term partnerships.
