What We Offer

Our Services

Comprehensive solutions across 10 key sectors, delivered with excellence and reliability.

All Services

From defense procurement to renewable energy, we deliver specialized solutions across every sector.

Defense & Security

Military-grade procurement specialists

Military equipment and tactical gear for Royal Armed Forces. We supply military-grade equipment, security systems, and tactical supplies to Oman's defense organizations, sourced from certified global manufacturers.

Oil & Gas

Upstream & downstream solutions

Industrial equipment, pipeline products, and MRO supplies for oil & gas operations. We supply the full range of equipment and materials for upstream and downstream operations from leading global suppliers with proven industry certifications.

Aviation

Ground support & aviation supplies

Aircraft MRO parts and ground support equipment for aviation operations. Trusted supplier to RAFO and Oman Air, delivering aircraft parts, maintenance tools, airside vehicles, and technical aviation supplies from internationally certified manufacturers.

Construction & Infrastructure

Materials & heavy equipment supply

Heavy machinery, equipment, and project management for construction and infrastructure projects. Our portfolio includes Caterpillar, Hitachi, JCB, and Volvo heavy equipment, as well as structural materials, electrical components, and civil engineering supplies.

Renewable Energy

Solar & sustainable energy solutions

Solar, wind, and energy storage solutions aligned with Oman Vision 2040. We provide complete renewable energy systems for residential, commercial, and industrial applications, partnering with leading manufacturers like Fuji Electric and Xtreme Power.

Marine

Vessel systems & offshore equipment

Marine equipment and vessel maintenance solutions for naval, commercial, and port operators. We supply marine systems, vessel components, navigation instruments, and offshore support equipment, with a proven track record serving the Royal Navy of Oman and ASYAD.

Printing Solutions

Industrial & commercial printing supplies

Industrial printing equipment and managed services for large-format, offset, and digital printing operations. We source toners, inks, spare parts, and printing machinery from leading international suppliers to keep your production running smoothly.

Health & Safety

PPE, safety & medical supplies

PPE, safety equipment, and fire suppression systems for workplace safety. We supply personal protective equipment, workplace safety systems, first-aid supplies, and occupational health products from accredited suppliers meeting international standards.

Logistics & General Trading

End-to-end supply chain solutions

Supply chain, freight forwarding, and warehousing — managing the full supply chain from procurement to final delivery. Services include product sourcing, customs clearance, freight coordination, and last-mile delivery across Oman, serving ASYAD, Mwasalat, and OQ.

Special Projects & Contracts

Tailored solutions for complex requirements

Turnkey projects and government contract management for complex procurement requirements. We handle specialized procurement projects, manage tenders, government RFQs, and multi-phase contracts with precision — ensuring on-time delivery, budget compliance, and full documentation.
